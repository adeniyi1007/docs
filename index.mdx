## Welcome to PeopleWisher

**PeopleWisher** helps you automate all your greetings — birthdays, anniversaries, milestones, festive messages, and even corporate appreciation — across **Email, SMS, WhatsApp, and more.**

From individuals to large organizations, PeopleWisher ensures no special moment is forgotten.
